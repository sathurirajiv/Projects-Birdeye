Birdeye Brand Guidelines

Version 2.4 · Confidential

Brand Mission

Birdeye helps businesses grow through customer experience. Our brand reflects trust, clarity, and modern professionalism — built for businesses that take their reputation seriously.

Logo Usage

Always use the primary blue logo on white or light backgrounds. Maintain a clear space of at least 16px around the logo on all sides. Never rotate, stretch, or recolor the logo.

Brand Colors

Primary Blue
#1976D2

Dark Blue

Page Grey
#F4F6F7

Typography

Primary typeface: Roboto. Use Regular (400) for body copy, Medium (500) for labels and UI elements, and Bold (700) for headlines. Minimum body size is 13px for digital and 10pt for print.

Email Guidelines

All customer-facing emails must include the Birdeye logo in the header, use brand blue (#1976D2) for CTAs, and maintain a single-column layout at 600px max width. Footer must include legal copy and unsubscribe link.

Voice & Tone

Be clear, concise, and helpful. Avoid jargon. Write in second person ("you", "your business"). Use active voice. Lead with the benefit, follow with the feature.
